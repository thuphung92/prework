Reasons to Become A Web Developer

Before moving to the US, I had never ever thought that I would do something related to programming. In stead, becoming an expert in Human Resources field was what I wanted to do.

Due to the language and culture barriers, it would take me much time to succeed in HR field. Meanwhile, I know that I am pretty good at Math and logical things. Therefore, I chose to learn something more technical to overcome those barriers. However, I was not one hundred percent sure to become a developer until I took my first required coding class about C++ at school. It was more enjoyable than what I thought, and I could feel that my mind was sharpen after a while when writing codes.

Although I am still a beginner at this point, I believe that I will gain a lot of knowledge after this bootcamp.
